Homecare

Take self-diagnosis one step further.

Never miss a step with Homecare

HomeCare is an app that allows patients to learn more about a musculoskeletal injury they may be suffering. It prompts the user to engage in several, simple physical exams, and then records whether the patient felt a lot of pain, some pain, or no pain using voice recognition technology.

As each question is answered, HomeCare technology is narrowing down the array of possible common injuries, giving each answer a score to be evaluated at the end. Once all questions are answered, it presents the injury with the highest score.

Finally, based on the possible diagnoses the users receive, HomeCare locates providers nearby using the BetterDoctor API.

Our Services

Ankle Assesment

Homecare assesses most of the common ankle injuries including:

  • inversion ankle sprain
  • eversion ankle sprain
  • achilles tendonitis
  • achilles tendon rupture
  • stress fracture
  • meniscus lateral tear

Knee Assessment

Knee injuries, like ankle injuries, are very debilitating. Here are some of the injuries we help diagnose:

  • ACL tear
  • MCL Tear
  • PCL Tear
  • LCL Tear
  • meniscus medial tear
